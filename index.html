About Us


The Tennis Mania shop is located in the heart of Dublin City on Winton Road, Ranelagh. Officially opened in November 2021, the shop is one of Dublin’s newest location for tennis racquets, clothing, footwear, accessories and restrings. The owners, Ryan, Daniel and Alexandru, transformed a neglected, rundown building into the tennis Mecca that it is today. Tennis Mania is now the only dedicated high-street tennis store in the country and maintains the only express racket restring service. Tennis Mania could not have gotten off the ground and been a success thus far, without the support of family and friends, a loyal customer base and fantastic staff! At Mania we are passionate about everything tennis so pop in to say Hi or for a coffee anytime you’re near by!
